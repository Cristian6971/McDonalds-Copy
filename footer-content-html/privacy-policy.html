Privacy Policy

Last update: 16.04.2024

Premier Restaurants Romania S.R.L. ("The Company" or "we"), based in Bucharest, America House Building, Șos. Nicolae Titulescu no. 4-8, West Wing, 5th Floor, Sector 1, is the operator of your personal data processed when you visit www.mcdonalds.ro ("Website") and our physical locations.

As a data controller, we continually monitor and ensure that the processing of personal data strictly complies with the principles and regulations regarding personal data protection, particularly the provisions of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data, and the repeal of Directive 95/46/EC (General Data Protection Regulation "GDPR").

The Company may update or modify this policy, and in such case, we will inform you by publishing the new version on the Website. The updated (if applicable, modified) Privacy Policy will take effect from the date of publication on the Website.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us by email at gdpr@ro.mcd.com or by mail or courier at the address in Bucharest, Șos. Nicolae Titulescu no. 4-8, America House Building, West Wing, 5th Floor, Sector 1.

This policy regulates how we manage your personal data and includes information such as:

  • Categories of personal data we process;
  • Purposes and legal bases for processing your personal data;
  • Retention period of this data;
  • Your rights as a data subject and how to exercise them;
  • Categories of entities to whom we may transfer your personal data (if applicable).

1. What categories of personal data does the Company process?

The categories of personal data we process depend on the context of your interactions and relationship with the Company and the Website. Therefore, the Company may process the following categories of personal data:

1.1. For Website Visitors

The personal data we process in this context are: the time and date of access to the Website and the IP address from which the Website was accessed. We use the personal data collected from you when accessing the Website to monitor traffic and improve the navigation experience. We have a legitimate interest in carrying out these processing activities to ensure the proper functioning of the Website and to improve it.

1.2. For Job Applicants

When you apply for a position at the Company by completing the form and uploading your CV on the Website in the "Join our team"/"Career" section, we process the following categories of personal data: the position you are applying for, name, surname, date of birth, the city where you wish to work, phone number, email address, preferred working hours, as well as optionally, your talents, skills, relevant experience, expected salary level, and information you choose to provide in your CV.

All the information you provide in this context will be processed exclusively for the purpose of processing your application and in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

1.3. For Contact Form Submissions

The personal data processed in this context are: name, email address, content of the message you send us, as well as optionally, your phone number and city.

1.4. For Participation in Marketing Campaigns

The personal data that may be processed in this context (depending on whether you are a non-winner or winner of the campaign and the specific terms of the campaign, in accordance with each campaign’s regulation) include: name, surname, email, phone, delivery address for the prize.

1.5. For Visiting Our Physical Locations (IP-CCTV System)

If you visit the Company’s headquarters or our restaurants and cafes inside buildings (including, for example, shopping malls) or on terraces, we may process your image captured by the IP-CCTV surveillance system, as detailed in the IP-CCTV Privacy Policy.

2. What are the purposes and legal bases for processing?

The Company processes your personal data for the purposes listed below, either directly or through authorized third parties:

  • To allow participation in our marketing campaigns and, if declared a winner, to send you the prize;
  • For necessary pre-employment procedures, including recruitment;
  • To ensure the proper functioning of the Website and improve it.

3. Data Retention Period

We retain your personal data exclusively for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or for compatible purposes. You can obtain more information on how long we retain your data by contacting us at our address in Bucharest.

4. How does the Company secure your personal data?

The Company takes the protection of your personal data seriously and implements the necessary technical and organizational measures to ensure that your personal data is protected. These measures ensure an appropriate level of security relative to the risks associated with the types of data processed and processing activities.

5. Who can the Company disclose your personal data to and why?

The Company is committed to protecting your personal data. For the purposes of processing, the Company may disclose certain categories of personal data to the following recipients:

  • Contractual partners providing services to the Company, such as Website management services;
  • Other companies in the group to which the Company belongs for potential joint marketing initiatives;
  • Public authorities, in response to their requests for information or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Company.
