About the company

McDonald's® is the largest quick service restaurant chain in the world. Over 32,000 restaurants, from over 100 countries, serve over 60 million people every day. We are always concerned with anticipating and responding to changes in the lives of customers, employees and the

McDonald's® system, through constant evolution and innovation.

Charity

The story of the Ronald McDonald® Children's Foundation began in 1974, in the corridors of a hospital in Philadelphia, USA. Today, there are more than 390 Ronald McDonald® Houses around the world. In Romania, the Ronald McDonald® Children's Foundation was established in

1998, opened three Ronald McDonald® Houses and supported many projects for the protection of sick children and their families.
