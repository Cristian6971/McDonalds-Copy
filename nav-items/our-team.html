Why choose a job at McDonald's®?

If you work in one of the McDonald's® restaurants, you enjoy the support you need, motivating salary, training and bonuses. Moreover, a job with us leaves you time for your dream, no matter what it is. Find out which role is right for you and apply for a position at your nearest McDonald's® restaurant.

Are you an energetic person, willing to accept new challenges and who wants to work alongside a dynamic team? Are you still a student and want to gain professional experience? Apply for the position of Crew Member and let's meet all the colleagues for whom the job at McDonald's® suits them!

As a Commercial Worker, you will handle a variety of activities that include all areas of a McDonald's® restaurant: kitchen, cash register and dining room..

Thus, you have the chance to develop your set of practical skills, but also your communication skills, following the interaction with customers.

Confused about working at McDonald's®? What does it entail or if you qualify?

From a legal point of view, you can be employed from the age of 16. Many young people want to earn money on their own and no longer depend on the help of their parents for any expenses. We encourage them to start working and we are always open to offer opportunities to those interested. In order to engage, however, you need your parents' consent.

Yes, you can apply for several different positions at the same time, but we will consider your requirements one at a time. We recommend that you read our job descriptions to help you decide which career path you want to pursue.

Our recommendation is the smart casual outfit. For example, a warm-colored T-shirt and dark-colored pants or skirt.

At the end of the recruitment process, if you have been selected for employment, you will receive a list of all the necessary documents in order to continue and complete the recruitment process.

Our restaurants operate within various time slots, up to 24 hours a day. For example, a morning shift can start at 6 o'clock. All our employees work in shifts, by rotation, which also means working the night shift (if you are over 18). The tours are designed to be to your advantage while taking into account the legislation in force.

At McDonald's® we place great emphasis on people development. Employees may advance based on on-the-job performance and demonstrated commitment by responsibly completing all position-related training programs. The salary package will be different depending on the position, according to the salary scale existing at that time.
